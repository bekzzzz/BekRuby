8 Days Migration Itinerary

Day 1 : Bishkek - Chon Kemin

Drive: 150km

The journey is expected to take around 2 hours. Our guest house in Chon Kemin is nestled next to the mountains, and we anticipate arriving by noon. Lunch will be served at the guest house, providing an opportunity to unwind and appreciate the beauty of the Chon Kemin Valley. After lunch, we'll embrace the valley's beauty with a 2-3 hour horseback rideâ€”a wonderful way to immerse ourselves in the stunning scenery and experience the tranquility of the area. In the evening learn more about kyrgyz traditional clothing: local aunty will come and show you Elechek traditional headdress.

Overnight: Jekshen guest house or Kok Archa guest house

Day 2 : Chon Keminâ€“Kochkor â€“ Naryn â€“ Shabyr

Drive: 280 km

Early morning drive from Chon Kemin to Central Kyrgyzstan, heading towards Naryn. On the way, we will stop to see traditional Kyrgyz carpet-making, a UNESCO heritage craft. Lunch will be served in Naryn city. From there, it's a two-hours drive to Shabyr yurt camp, followed by a walk through the forest to the camp. Check into the yurts and enjoy dinner at the camp.

Overnight: Shabyr yurt camp

Day 3 : Shabyr â€“ Song Kol The day of Nomad migration

Drive: 50km off road

  • Begin with an early visit to a nomadic family's camp, witnessing cattle counting.
  • Share a traditional breakfast with the family.
  • Help pack the yurt and belongings into a GAZelle car.
  • Depart for a scenic drive to the summer pasture (jailoo).
  • Upon arrival, assist in setting up the yurts and learn about nomadic architecture.
  • Participate in the "Jyt Chygaruu" ceremony: cooking, wishing for safe passage and favorable conditions during migrations. Enjoy a hearty lunch with the family, fostering connections and sharing stories.
  • Conclude the day by bidding farewell to the nomadic family and expressing gratitude.
  • Transfer to your lakeside yurt camp to relax and enjoy the surroundings.

    • Overnight: Mirlan or Baiysh yurt camp

    Day 4 : Song Kol â€“ Bokonbayevo village

    Drive: 250km (100 km off road)

    By noon at 11 am we will drive to Bokonbayevo village to explore the Issyk-Kul region, starting with the south shore. On the way, we'll visit Teskey Torpok waterfall and have lunch in Kochkor village. After lunch, an eagle man will demonstrate traditional Kyrgyz eagle hunting with a golden eagle. We'll then relax by the Issyk-Kul Lakeâ€”feel free to swim or meditate by the mountain lake. Dinner will be served at the guest house.

    Overnight: Emily guest house or Altyn Oimok

    Day 5 : Bokonbayevo-Karakol

    Drive: 150 km

    After breakfast we will drive to Fairy tail canyons to walk between red rocks in the morning lights. By noon we will arrive at Barskoon village for a yurt set up with Meken ava- he is the one the best yurt producers in Kyrgyzstan. Lunch in Meken avaâ€™s house. After lunch we will drive to Jeti oguz( 7 bulls canyon) to see global lift up traces. By evening you will arrive in Karakol city visit Dungan mosque and Orthodox church. Dinner at ethno restaurant.

    Overnight: Matsunoki Japanese House

    Day 6 : Karakol â€“ Altyn Arashan- Karakol

    Drive: 50 km off road

    Today we have an adventure to Altyn arashan valley-we call it the land of hot springs. The Russian military car will pick you up at 8 am, drive will take around 2 hours, it's a very stony, bumpy off road drive, please prepare for this adventure. Upon arrival we will have lunch in the mountain house. Afternoon time to hike around the valley ( around 2 hours). By coming to the basecamp, I swim in a hot spring. By evening, will back to Karakol city and stay in the same Japanese house.

    Overnight: Matsunoki Japanese House

    Day 7 : Karakol â€“ Cholpon ata

    Drive: 150 km â€“ driving

    Today we will explore the north shore of Issyk-Kul Lake. This side is more westernized, as most Russian people settled here during the Soviet Union era in the 20th century. Afternoon, we will take a cruise with views of the snowy mountains. If you want you can swim in the mountain lake. Chill time in the evening on the beach. Dinner at a local restaurant.

    Overnight: Olimp hotel

    Day 8 : Cholpon ata - Bishkek

    Drive: 270 km

    On our last day of the tour, we'll drive from Cholpon Ata to Bishkek. We will have lunch on the way and visit Osh Bazaar to buy local dried fruits and souvenirs. After checking into the hotel, we'll enjoy a farewell dinner at Apaâ€™s house.
